ABOUT US

Who We Are

At TC Group, we are passionate about transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments. With years of experience in the home improvement, renovation, and interior design industry, we have become a trusted name across Gauteng, Mpumalanga, and Limpopo provinces.

Our commitment to quality craftsmanship, innovative design, and attention to detail allows us to deliver spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful. From residential homes to commercial spaces, we bring a personal touch to every project, ensuring our clients’ visions come to life.



Where We Work

We take pride in serving clients across a broad range of locations. Our projects span from Gauteng to Mpumalanga and Limpopo provinces, covering areas such as Midrand, Vaal, Soweto, Sandton, Cosmo, Protea Glen, Johannesburg, Pretoria, Benoni, Mabopane, Bramley, Roodepoort, and beyond—the list is endless. No matter where you are, TC Group is ready to bring our expertise to your doorstep.

Our Promise to You

At the core of TC Group are the following values, which guide every project we undertake:


  • Excellence: We deliver top-quality craftsmanship in every project.
  • Integrity: Honesty and transparency guide all our interactions.
  • Customer Focus: We tailor our designs to meet each client’s unique needs.

  • Sustainability: We prioritize eco-friendly practices and materials.


Our Purpose

Our purpose is to transform dreams into reality by delivering high-quality, bespoke home and office solutions that inspire and enhance everyday living. We are dedicated to providing exceptional craftsmanship, personalized service, and timeless designs that add value and beauty to any space.

TCG Vision

To be the leading home improvement and interior design company in South Africa, renowned for our ability to blend luxury, comfort, and functionality in every project. We aim to continuously innovate, grow, and expand our reach, becoming the go-to name for top-tier renovations and custom furnishings.

We pride ourselves on being a comprehensive, turnkey service provider in the home improvement and interior design industry!
